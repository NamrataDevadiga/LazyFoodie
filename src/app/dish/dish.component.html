Gajar Ka Halwa (Carrot Halwa)

Gajar ka Halwa, also known as carrot halwa or Gajrela is an Indian dessert or sweet which is a winter delicacy. Gajar Halwa is made with juicy red carrots, milk sugar and a lot of dry fruits and nuts. The recipe is simple and super easy though it requires a lot of patience because of the slow cooking.

Preperation Time: 30 minutes

Cooking Time: 60 minutes

Ingridients: 1 kg grated carrots, 1 lit Milk, 2 black Cardamom, 5 tbsp Sugar, Almonds, 1 cup Condensed Milk, 2 Tbsp Ghee

How to cook Gajar ka Halwa:

  1. Bring the milk to a slight boil and add carrots. Now reduce the heat to low and allow the carrots to cook and milk is reduced.
  2. Keep stirring on regular intervals for 30 minutes so it does not burn and stick to the bottom.
  3. When all the liquid is evaporated and milk is reduced/solidified add sugar and again cook till juices/melted sugar is again reduced.
  4. At this stage add the condensed milk and cook some more for 5-6 minutes till it is well incorporated into mix.
  5. Now in a separate deep pan add ghee and heat it, when ghee is hot transfer cooked halwa and cook it for 1 minutes and keep stirring it all the while.
  6. After the halwa is cooked add mixed dry fruits and nuts of your choice. serve it hot and garnish with almonds for a sweet treat.
